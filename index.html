Our Vision & Mission

To facilitate compassionate, equitable, whole-person health and wellness services on Anderson Island provided by PCHS. We are dedicated to improving quality of life by sourcing exceptional primary care, advancing optimal health outcomes, and fostering community engagement.


How?

PCHS.

PCHS brings the clinic to you, where you can see a Nurse, Primary Care Doctor, and more right here.

The van will be dispatched on a schedule based on our budget and preferences, and will arrive at a destination of our choosing and await your visit.

AIHA has answers

We have been listening and asking a ton of questions to get where we are. So here are the most frequently asked questions (FAQs)
already answered for you.

If you're still scratching your head, ask away! Click Here to Contact Us

How do I sign up to receive services?

Fill out our form here and let's get in touch!

What if I'm not a resident?

We serve everyone who needs care on Anderson Island. Welcome aboard!

How does it work?

Services begin once AIHA raises sufficient funds to underwrite each van day on the island.

Target: fall launch once fundraising goals are reached.

Is there financial aid?

We offer various plans and support options. Let's chat about what fits.

Why this approach?

After exploring multiple options, a mobile clinic is the most practical and cost-effective way to begin care on the island.

Who is providing care?

PCHS is under contract with AIHA to staff and operate the mobile van. They are a federally qualified health center with deep experience serving rural communities.

Can I donate?

Absolutely! Every bit helps keep care local.
